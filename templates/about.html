About Us

At de_TECT, we offer a cutting-edge detection system that integrates object, lane, and curve recognition to enhance driving safety and precision. Our technology uses advanced computer vision and AI to provide real-time awareness of obstacles, lane markings, and road curves, supporting both driver assistance and autonomous driving solutions. With our system, you gain a smarter, safer driving experience on every journey

How the System works

Object Detection

Input

The system takes images or video frames from cameras.

Processing

It uses machine learning models to identify and classify objects within the images

Output

The system provides information about the location, type, and sometimes the movement of detected objects (like vehicles, pedestrians, or traffic signs).

Lane and Curve Detection

Input

The system takes images or video frames from cameras.

Processing

The system employs image processing techniques to detect lane markings on the road and the system analyzes the road curvature by detecting and interpreting lane markings and road features.

Output

The system identifies lane boundaries and can provide feedback on lane position, helping with lane-keeping and lane departure warnings. It also provides information about the roadâ€™s curvature, aiding in steering adjustments and improving vehicle handling on curves.
